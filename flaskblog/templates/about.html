About This Web Application

Welcome to my Flask web application!

The web app features:

  • A blog page where users can share posts.
  • A hangman game for some light-hearted fun.
  • A fitness tracker for users to log their workouts.

About Me

I'm David Cain, an Electrical Engineer Project Manager with a master's degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette.

I specialize in designing embedded systems that require analog and digital processing systems for a wide variety of hardware.

My expertise includes low-level register programming, FPGA development using VHDL and Verilog, embedded C/C++ (both bare metal and within FreeRTOS), Python, and Flutter applications.

This web app is part of my ongoing journey to explore and master Flask and Python, with each feature serving as a practical way to deepen my understanding of web development.
